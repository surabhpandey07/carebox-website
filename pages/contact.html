FAQ

How can I contact CareBox?

Email/LinkedIn: sales@careboxhealthcare.com or https://www.linkedin.com/company/carebox-heathcare-solutions-llp/posts/?feedView=all Live Chat: You can also drop a message in the chat box on the website, where an AI assistant will assist you with your queries.

How to request a Demo?

Tap on the ‘Demo’ tab mentioned on the banner. Fill up the form with your interest and details, and the company will contact you to schedule a demo. You can also directly contact us on Linkedin / Email.

What to expect during the demo from Carebox?

At CareBox, we would be delighted to guide you through all aspects of our healthcare solutions, from functionality and user experience to customization options, support, and training. We’ll help you explore pricing and licensing, ensure compliance with security regulations, and demonstrate the effectiveness and benefits our solution can bring to your healthcare needs. Whatever your requirements are, we are here to support you every step of the way.

How can one install your app?

One of our expert executives will either visit your location to oversee the installation of software or provide remote support to guide your team through the process.

Is your software compatible with both Windows & Mac?

Yes, our software is made to be completely compatible with both Windows and Mac operating systems. We ensure the software works seamlessly across multiple platforms so users can access it regardless of their preferred OS. If you need specific customization for one platform over another, we can tailor the solution to meet your needs.

Can the software be used on multiple devices, including smartphones?

Yes! It can be used on multiple devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktops. It can be optimized for seamless use on various platforms, ensuring that healthcare professionals and patients can access data and services anywhere and anytime on their preferred devices.

How can I get technical support?

You can reach us through our official email or contact our executive, who initially helped you with the installation process. Our expert executive will guide you with all the technical challenges that healthcare management deals with.

How can I receive updates about new products & offers?

You can sign up for our email newsletters, push notifications of our app, and SMS alerts that notify you directly about our new products and deals.

How will the software reduce workload and save time for staff?

Carebox helps streamline the entire healthcare management process from back office to front office procedures. It saves time by automating monotonous and tedious administrative tasks like patient scheduling, billing, documentation, data entry, communication, and more, allowing healthcare professionals to focus on direct patient care and crucial activities rather than manual paperwork and data management processes.

Can the software integrate with other software and devices?

Yes! It can absolutely integrate with other software and devices, allowing for smooth data sharing and improved patient care by connecting various IT systems, applications, and medical devices within a healthcare organization. It helps in reducing manual data entry, enhances workflow efficiency, enables real-time access to patient information, and improves decision-making quality.

How does the software facilitate data interchange and security?

It essentially does with (EDI) Electronic Data Interchange that allows secure data exchange of patient information between various healthcare entities like hospitals, insurance companies, and labs by utilizing established message formats and encryption protocols. It ensures the confidentiality of data while complying with regulatory requirements.

How secure is the patient data?

Patient data security is our top priority. Our healthcare software adheres to the strictest security standards and regulations, to ensure that patient information is protected. We implement robust data encryption (both in transit and at rest), secure authentication methods (like multi-factor authentication), and role-based access controls to ensure that only authorized personnel can access sensitive data. We also maintain audit logs tracking access to patient data and conduct regular security audits to identify and address potential vulnerabilities. Our software provides a high level of security, helping to safeguard patient confidentiality and maintain trust.

Is the system password protected?

Our system is password-protected to ensure that only authorized users can access it. In addition to password protection, we implement multi-factor authentication (MFA) for an added layer of security. It requires users to verify their identity through an additional method, such as a one-time code or biometric authentication, further protecting patient data from unauthorized access.

How does the software comply with standards and security policies?

Our software is designed to meet and exceed industry standards and regulatory requirements, ensuring the highest data protection and privacy. We implement strong security measures like data encryption, access controls, audit trails, and regular security audits. Our system also incorporates role-based access control (RBAC) to limit data access to authorized personnel based on their roles and responsibilities. Additionally, we stay up-to-date with evolving standards and continuously update our security protocols to adapt to new threats and regulatory changes

Is the software user-friendly?

It's user-friendly software with an easy-to-navigate interface to ensure that our healthcare professionals can access patient data efficiently without any technical difficulties. Our design process involves user feedback and testing to create a system that minimizes complexity and enhances productivity. Additionally, we offer comprehensive training and support to ensure that users can quickly become comfortable with the software and maximize its features.

Does the software have self-help features like tooltips or guided tutorials?

Yes, our software includes self-help features such as tooltips, guided tutorials, and in-app help documentation to assist users in navigating the system. These features provide real-time guidance, explaining key functions and offering step-by-step instructions to help users complete the tasks. Additionally, we provide video tutorials and FAQ resources to ensure users have everything they need for a smooth experience.

How easy is it for staff to learn and navigate the software?

The staff can quickly learn and navigate the system, as the intuitive interface and workflows make it easy for users to understand key functions without extensive training. To further support the staff, we offer comprehensive training programs, guided tutorials, and interactive support tools. Our self-help features like tooltips and in-app guidance, help users find the information they need in real time. With these resources, most staff members can become proficient with the software quickly and confidently.

How can a patient reschedule or cancel the appointment?

Patients can easily reschedule or cancel their appointments through the patient portal or mobile app, depending on the system used. Simply log in to the portal or app, navigate to the Appointments section, and select the option to reschedule or cancel an existing appointment. In some cases, patients may also receive reminders via email or SMS with links to manage their appointments directly. If further assistance is needed, patients can contact the office staff through the portal or via phone.

Can patients book appointments for family members through their accounts?

Yes, the patients can book appointments for their family members by logging into their account, selecting - the ‘Book for a Family Member’ Option, providing details, choosing the appointment type, select the date & time to confirm the appointment.

If the hospital experiences technical issues with the system, how do you plan to solve it?

In the event of technical issues, our team is committed to resolving the problem quickly and efficiently. We offer 24/7 technical support through multiple channels, including phone, email, and live chat. Our support team works closely with the hospital’s IT staff to diagnose and troubleshoot the issue, ensuring minimal disruption to operations. We also provide system monitoring to proactively identify and address potential issues before they affect functionality. If necessary, we can implement software updates or patches to fix any bugs or security vulnerabilities
