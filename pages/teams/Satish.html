Team Introduction
Home/Team

Mr. Manish Sheth brings over three decades of experience in Healthcare IT Systems, Project Management, and Software Solutions, alongside a wealth of visionary leadership expertise to Carebox Healthcare Solutions LLP . A Chinai College of Commerce and Economics graduate , he has built an influential career in Healthcare and Finance, bridging the gaps between Healthcare Management, Technology Solutions, and Finance.

 

As a CEO , Mr. Sheth plays a pivotal role in driving the development of the company and implementing cutting-edge software solutions across various sectors. His deep understanding of Charitable Hospitals, Medical College Hospitals, and Corporate Hospitals has been instrumental in improving the healthcare ecosystem and enhancing operational efficiencies.

 

Mr. Sheth has successfully managed and coordinated large-scale healthcare projects, overseeing all phases from initiation through planning and execution. His proven track record of implementing healthcare software solutions in numerous large hospitals showcases his unmatched ability to drive transformation and deliver scale-based value.

 

In addition to his healthcare expertise, Mr. Sheth’s strong background in Finance and Accounting further strengthens his ability to manage projects from financial and operational perspectives. His enthusiasm adds energy to the workplace, fostering a collaborative environment, while his exceptional multitasking skills make him an invaluable asset to Carebox Healthcare Solutions LLP.

Manish Sheth
CEO - Product & Development
