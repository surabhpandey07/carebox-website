Ajay Sheth
Chairman


With over 40 years of experience in Information Technology and 35 years specializing in healthcare systems, Mr. Ajay Sheth is one of the pioneering minds behind India’s first HIMS (Hospital Information Management System), a recognized expert, and a leading authority in integrating advanced IT solutions to modernize the healthcare sector.

 

A postgraduate from Mumbai University, Mr. Ajay’s expertise spans financial analysis and strategic planning, focusing on cost control, decision-making, and systems design. He strongly focuses on developing economic systems that foster efficiency and stability. His passion for modernizing healthcare through Information and Communication Technology (ICT), along with his leadership, has been instrumental in shaping the future of healthcare systems..

 

As a Chairman of CAREBOX Healthcare Solutions, Mr. Sheth uses his vast experience and vision to drive innovative solutions that enhance operational efficiency and improve patient care. His deep understanding of financial systems and proficiency in systems design allow him to create robust frameworks that optimize operational performance and monetary outcomes for healthcare organizations.

 

Having successfully implemented systems in over 100 healthcare organizations —including hospitals, laboratories, clinics, and medical facilities, makes him deeply knowledgeable about industry best practices. His profound experience in healthcare system design and optimization enables him to create impactful solutions that deliver exceptional returns and long-term benefits for healthcare providers.

 

Mr. Sheth's charismatic leadership fosters a collaborative and innovative environment, instilling pride within the team and driving them to deliver effective solutions for the healthcare sector.

A progressive firm driven to provide pioneering healthcare solutions to enhance the patient experience with a wide range of services designed to streamline healthcare operations and a focus on efficiency, accessibility, and patient-centered care.
